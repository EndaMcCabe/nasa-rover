What is NASA

NASA aka National Aeronautics and Space Administration. The started for business in 1958 after the Space Age started when Soviet satellite Sputnik launched the year prior.

What Does Nasa Do:

Not a lot of people know what exactly NASA does. They have astronauts on the ISS that are conducting scientific research. They plan to send people back to the moon and eventually to Mars.
