What is the Mars Rover

Mars Rover is a project from NASA. It's purpose was to send a vehicle to Mars and to be able to assess the surface of Mars.

When did Mars Rover land on Mars

Mars Rover landed on August 6th 2012 at Mount Sharp.

How recent is the most recent Mission

The most recent mission launched on July 30 2020, named Perseverance.
